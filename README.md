# Porto Parking Prototype

This prototype was developed as part of the **Erasmus+ BIP Data and AI 2026** program, in collaboration with **Fontys ICT**.

The main challenge was to apply Data and Artificial Intelligence concepts to ideate Smart City solutions for real urban problems.

## The Concept: Reducing Traffic in Porto
Our proposal focuses on restructuring mobility in the city of Porto. The core idea is based on three pillars:
1. **Removal of Central Parking:** Eliminating public parking spaces in the city center streets to free up urban space.
2. **Peripheral Hubs:** Redirecting private cars to large parking facilities located outside the city center.
3. **Robotaxi Network:** Utilizing a fleet of autonomous vehicles to provide efficient and continuous connections between the peripheral hubs and the inner city.

## About the Prototype
The strategic map was built with Next.js and consumes real open data provided by [opendata.porto.digital](https://opendata.porto.digital/). The mapping includes strategic locations in GeoJSON format, such as EV charging zones, metro stations, and tariffed areas, to simulate the feasibility of implementing robotaxis.

You can view the deployed prototype [here](https://porto-parking-prototype.vercel.app/).
